Registration

To download the ICCSE2019 Registration Notice please click the link below



Registration steps for ICCSE 2019:

  • 1.Please login the EasyChair online system (http://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=iccse2019) to upload the final camera ready paper.
  • 2.Please pay the registration fee (Before June 1, 2019).
  • 3.Fill in the registration form online through http://ieee-iccse.org/registration.html or send it back to iccse.2019@gmail.com and iccse2019@163.com (Before June 1, 2019).

1.Upload the final camera ready paper and transfer the E-copyright to IEEE.

(1) Upload the camera-ready paper

a.Revise your paper, if applicable, according to the reviewers’ comments

b.Use the IEEE Submission Templates for Conference Proceedings to reformat your papers.

Templates can be downloaded here:

https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html

c.PDF Test. ICCSE will only accept papers in PDF format, which have passed IEEE PDF eXpress Online test.

Help: http://www.ieee.org/web/publications/pubservices/confpub/pdfexpress.html

Tool: http://pdf-express.org/plus/ (Conference code: 46734xp)

d.Upload your paper by June 1, 2019. Please login the EasyChair, click the “Update file” on the upper right corner to process.

After confirmation of the title, abstract, and authors list, you can upload the final camera-ready paper.

(2)Transfer the Copyright to IEEE.

After you finish step (1), please write us an email with a title “Your paper ID, Authors’ Name List” to declare you will transfer the copyright of your submission to IEEE.

The email address is iccse.2019@gmail.com and iccse2019@163.com. The conference will help to upload the copyright information to IEEE.
